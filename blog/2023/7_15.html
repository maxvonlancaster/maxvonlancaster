Ideas for student group projects

15th of July, 2023

Ideas for student group projects for the course "Modern web-tech development"

  • Study Buddy: An app that allows students to connect with each other for studying and sharing resources. It could include features like study groups, file sharing, and chat functionality.
  • Task Tracker: A simple app for managing and tracking tasks and assignments. Students can create to-do lists, set reminders, and mark tasks as completed.
  • Time Management Tool: An app that assists in managing productivity time effectively. It could include features like setting work schedules, creating reminders, and tracking time spent on different tasks.
  • Budget Planner: An app that helps users track their expenses, set budgets, and manage their finances. It could include features like expense categorization, monthly summaries, and notifications for overspending.
  • College Marketplace: A platform for students to buy and sell items within their college community. It could include categories for textbooks, furniture, electronics, and other student necessities.
  • College Event Calendar: A centralized platform for students to discover and keep track of events happening on campus, such as workshops, seminars, and social gatherings. It could include event details, RSVP functionality, and reminders.
  • Grade Calculator: An app that helps students calculate their grades based on different assignments, exams, and weightings. It could also provide insights into how different scores would impact their overall grades.
  • Virtual Lab Assistant: An interactive app that simulates laboratory experiments for science and engineering students. It allows them to perform virtual experiments, collect data, and analyze results.
  • Flashcard Generator: An app that allows students to create digital flashcards for studying different subjects. It could include features like importing images, organizing flashcards into decks, and tracking progress.
  • Internship/Job Board: A platform that aggregates internship and job opportunities specifically targeted at students. It provides a centralized hub for students to find and apply for relevant positions based on their interests and qualifications.
  • Book Exchange Platform: A web app that enables users to trade or lend books with other students in their neighborhood. It could include features like book listings, search filters, and messaging capabilities.
  • Student Feedback System: An app that enables students to provide feedback on courses, professors, and other aspects of their educational experience. It could include anonymous feedback options, rating systems, and comment sections.
  • Virtual Career Fair: An app that brings together employers and students in a virtual setting. It allows companies to showcase job/internship opportunities, conduct interviews, and provide resources for career development.
  • Mental Health Support: An app focused on promoting mental health and well-being. It could include features like guided meditation, mood tracking, stress management techniques, and access to mental health resources.
  • Group Project Manager: An app designed to facilitate group projects by helping students assign tasks, set deadlines, and collaborate effectively. It could include features like file sharing, task tracking, and communication tools.
  • Personalized Book Recommendation: Description: Build a book recommendation app that suggests personalized book lists based on the user's reading preferences, ratings, and genres of interest.
  • Food Waste Reduction: Description: Develop an app to reduce food waste by connecting users with nearby restaurants, cafes, or grocery stores offering surplus food at discounted prices before it goes to waste.
  • Dream Journal with AI Analysis: Description: Create a dream journal app where users can record their dreams. The app uses natural language processing and AI to analyze dream patterns and meanings.
  • Music Collaboration Platform: Description: Develop a platform for musicians and artists to collaborate on music projects virtually, allowing them to record, mix, and produce music together.
  • Local Community Exchange Platform: Description: Create a platform where members of a local community can offer and exchange goods, services, and skills with each other, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.
  • Language Learning Game with AI: Description: Build an interactive language learning game that uses AI to adapt to each user's language proficiency and provide personalized lessons and challenges.
  • Real-Time Collaborative Code Editor: Description: Develop a collaborative code editor that allows multiple users to work on the same codebase simultaneously, promoting real-time collaboration among developers.
